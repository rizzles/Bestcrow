A Bitcoin Escrow Service. No Account Needed.

Bitcoin offers for the first time in the history of the world the ability to secure funds in a safe escrow account for any size transaction. In the global economy of the internet, purchase from anyone with confidence.

Escrow funds are stored on the blockchain

Your funds are stored in a multi signature bitcoin address while they are in escrow.
Enterprise level security

We utilize hierachal deterministic bitcoin wallets. The private keys to your escrow address don’t exist until we need them to move the funds out of escrow.

Monitor your funds

The bitcoin address is public on the blockchain. You can utilize public websites such as blockchain.info to monitor your funds while in escrow.

No account neccessary

When a escrow account is started, you are assigned a unique web page address for that transaction. Keep the address URL to be able to complete the transaction.

Dispute resolution

We offer dispute resolution if any stage of the transaction is not to your liking
Buyer protection

The funds are always sent to the seller in a standard transaction. But if you have a major problem as a buyer, open a dispute with us and we will gather as much information as possible and potentially return funds to the buyer.

Seller protection

As a seller, you can monitor the funds in the public bitcoin address used for escrow. You don’t need to ship/sell the item until you know the escrow account has been funded.

Need more security?

Use our native OSX and Windows apps to store the private key for your multi-sig bitcoin escrow address.
Local backups

A hierarchal deterministic wallet is used in the creation of the private keys in the native app. Copy 12 word phrase down when the app is launched to be able to recover your escrow transactions from a local disaster.
